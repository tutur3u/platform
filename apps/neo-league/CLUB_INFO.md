# About Neo Culture Tech

## Who We Are
Neo Culture Tech (NCT) is a vibrant and dynamic student club at RMIT University Vietnam, dedicated to fostering a passion for technology and innovation within the university community. We believe in the power of technology to shape the future and empower individuals to make a positive impact on the world.

## Our Mission
We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone feels empowered to learn, grow, and contribute their unique talents. Our goal is to provide a platform for students to:
- Explore their interests in various tech fields.
- Connect with like-minded individuals.
- Develop valuable skills through hands-on projects, workshops, and engaging events.

## What We Do
We are a dynamic platform dedicated to empowering the tech community through:
- **Engaging Workshops:** Providing practical skills and knowledge in various domains (e.g., Tech Workshops on GitHub, Arduino, and Soft Skills).
- **Thought-Provoking Talk Shows:** Featuring prominent figures in the industry to offer valuable insights.
- **Strategic Collaborations:** Partnering with leading tech companies to provide access to cutting-edge technologies, industry resources, and career opportunities.

By combining these initiatives, we create a vibrant ecosystem that fosters innovation, drives growth, and connects technophiles.

## Our Community
Beyond technology, we believe in building lasting relationships and memories. Our events are more than just learning opportunities; they are a chance to connect and have fun.
- **Neo Connect Series**
- **Internal & Food Bonding:** Relaxed gatherings to mingle and network.
- **Sports & Activities:** From the NCT FC football team to badminton tournaments and "Neo Escape" events.
